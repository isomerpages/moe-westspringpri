### PLAY through English

#### Teaching & Learning Approach

_**Learn** the English Language_  
_**Master** the language for effective communication with others_  
_**Succeed** in becoming an effective and confident user of the English Language_

At West Spring, our students find enjoyment in the learning of the English language through the five main domains of PLAY (Purposeful Learning Activities for the Young) learning approach – Physical PLAY, Dramatic PLAY, Object PLAY, ICT PLAY and Sand PLAY.
